About Us

We are the University of Queensland’s founding Biotechnology Student Society. We aim to provide professional networking, employability opportunities, research information, social events for science students, and mentoring – all to enhance the student experience. We are a collective of ambitious students from across biotechnology and STEM who want to connect with like-minded people, build relationships, discover the world of biotech, and engage with industry leaders.
